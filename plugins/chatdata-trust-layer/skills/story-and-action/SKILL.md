# Story And Action

Every material ChatData answer should include:

- what changed
- why it matters
- what evidence supports it
- what is still uncertain
- what decision or action is recommended
- owner
- success metric
- check-in date
- fallback if the recommendation is wrong

Prefer concise operator language. Lead with the answer, then evidence and caveats. Do not turn the output into a methodology lecture.
