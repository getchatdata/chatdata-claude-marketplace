# Metric Trust Packets

Every important metric should become a ChatData metric trust packet.

Required fields:

- metric name and business question
- plain-English definition and formula
- numerator, denominator, grain, timezone
- required filters and exclusions
- business owner and data owner
- blessed dashboard, query, model, report, or semantic object
- source freshness expectation
- known caveats and unreliable segments
- validation tolerance
- approved answer paths
- clarify, refuse, and escalate rules
- review state: draft, benchmarked, verified, trusted

If multiple definitions exist, do not pick silently. Flag the ambiguity and route the metric to owner review.
